      1. {% blocktranslate trimmed %} Rewards: throughout the DAAZ journey, practical or summary content and a certificate of achievement must be unlocked to be downloaded as rewards.         {% blocktranslate trimmed %} Each level allows you to unlock, 4 practical sheets, 1 summary sheet, a level report and 1 template document. {% endblocktranslate %}

        1. {% blocktranslate trimmed %} A practical sheet is a document on a specific topic, to draw attention to important aspects of the GDPR. Their content is not exhaustive.
          To obtain it, it is necessary to answer one of the 4 quizzes of the level and to validate the selected answer(s). {% endblocktranslate %}
        2. {% blocktranslate trimmed %} A summary sheet is a document summarizing in a structured way, the explanations provided for each of the 20 questions. Their content is not exhaustive.
          To obtain it, it is necessary to complete a level in its entirety. {% endblocktranslate %}
        3. {% blocktranslate trimmed %} A Template document is a document provided to allow you to document your compliance with the GDPR. It must be completed adequately according to the treatments carried out. To obtain it, it is necessary to wait for the âChallengeâ at the end of the level. {% endblocktranslate %}
        4. {% blocktranslate trimmed %} A level report is a document transcribing all the answers given and the score obtained by level.
          To obtain it, a level must be completed in its entirety. {% endblocktranslate %}
        5. {% blocktranslate trimmed %} A certificate of completion is the document that validates the completion of the DAAZ journey. It is, in a way, the diploma attesting to your overall knowledge of the principles and obligations of the GDPR acquired throughout the DAAZ.
          To obtain it, you must complete the DAAZ journey in its entirety. {% endblocktranslate %}

      2. {% translate "Result" %} :

        1. {% blocktranslate trimmed %} The Score is based on your total of correct answers to the 20 questions and 4 quizzes for each of the 4 levels of the journey.
          Each correct answer is worth 1 point.
          A single choice question brings 1 point.
          A multiple choice answer brings as many points as there are correct answers to select for this type of question.
          A wrong answer does not bring a negative point.
          {% endblocktranslate %}

        2. {% blocktranslate trimmed %} Dashboard stickers are scalable based on score.
          Once 70%% of correct answers are acquired, a pennant unlocks and appears on the image.
          Once 90%% of correct answers are acquired, golden stickers unlock.
          {% endblocktranslate %}

      3. {% blocktranslate trimmed %} Knowledge: the percentage of knowledge is acquired and increases throughout the DAAZ journey, after each explanation, each practice sheet unlocked and template document unlocked at the time of the end-of-level challenge. {% endblocktranslate %}

      4. {% blocktranslate trimmed %} journey: the percentage of achievement per level changes as you progress through the DAAZ journey. {% endblocktranslate %}
